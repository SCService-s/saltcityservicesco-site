Services

Three ways to engage

Each engagement is scoped to a specific outcome with a defined deliverable at the end.

01

Workflow Audit

A structured assessment of your current operations. I map what your team actually does, identify the manual patterns creating the most drag, and deliver a prioritized action plan. You know exactly what to fix first and why.

02

Custom Script & Automation Build

One specific problem, automated. Google Apps Script built around the tools you already use. Whether it is data flow between Sheets and Zendesk, auto-generated reports, or eliminating a manual copy-paste step, the output is a deployed script with documentation.

03

Zendesk Administration

Full configuration or a targeted tune-up. Triggers, automations, macros, AI bot logic, queue routing, and Help Center architecture. Built for how your team actually works, not the default setup no one ever changed.
